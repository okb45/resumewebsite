About Me

Hello! My name is Owen Bates. I am a striving Computer Engineer at the Rochester Institute of Technology, graduating in December 2025 and seeking opportunities starting January 2026.

Over the past few years, I’ve gained hands-on experience in agricultural robotics, working on systems that integrate GNSS positioning, embedded controllers, and sensor-driven automation to support real-world field applications.

My biggest passion lies in embedded engineering! Building efficient, reliable systems that bridge hardware and software. From implementing variable rate applications in agriculture to microcontroller game emulation with FPGA accelerated graphics, I enjoy tackling the full stack of challenges in embedded design.

I’m eager to bring my technical skills and enthusiasm for problem-solving into a collaborative engineering role where I can continue learning and contributing to innovative projects.
